Smart Navigation Features

Experience seamless campus navigation with our advanced features

Real-time Indoor Positioning

Accurate indoor navigation using BLE beacons with precision up to 3 meters, helping you find any room or facility instantly.

QR Code Scanning

Scan QR codes placed across campus for instant directions to your destination with just a tap.

Voice-assisted Navigation

Hands-free navigation with voice guidance in multiple languages, perfect for when you're on the move.

Schedule Integration

Sync your class schedule and get automatic reminders with directions to your next class location.

Real-time Updates

Get instant notifications about room changes, cancelled classes, or special events affecting your schedule.

Social Features

Find friends on campus, share your location for meetups, and discover popular student hangouts.
